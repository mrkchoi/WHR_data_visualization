Happiest Cities in America

Social scientists and researchers are moving away from traditional metrics such as GDP to rank regional quality of living. Other factors like family, friends, entertainment and work, may play a greater role in determining how joyful our lives actually are.

This 2019 study, conducted by NerdWallet, aggregates 31 key metrics including depression rates, income-growth rate and average leisure time spent per day, to rank the happiest cities in the United States.
